Smart Facilities Management

Explore how Proccoli’s cloud-based software can help your business improve productivity and enhance service delivery, be it a small business or enterprise campus. The software platform integrates systems, processes, technologies and personnel to enhance the management of any building’s facilities.

Data Driven Decisions

Proccoli’s Mobile Application or Telegram Bots ensures data integration from supplier and residents respectively to maintain accuracy and consistency of data which can help Facilities Managers make timely and informed decisions, create change and improve operational outcomes at lower costs in the longer term.

Integrated Digital Ecosystems

A single platform connecting all stakeholders - Facilities Management Companies, Facilities Manager, Estate Councils, Residents and Suppliers. The integration creates a seamless and end-to-end user experience that places your customers’ experience first. By accumulating, sharing, analysing and acting upon the collective data across users we help you go from good to great.

Integrated Predictive Maintenance strategy

In collaboration with industry experts, Proccoli provides maintenance teams with the ability to predict the remaining useful life of a part or an asset based on real-time data gives organizations an unprecedented way to manage and optimize their maintenance resources.
